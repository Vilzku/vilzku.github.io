By nature, I am very meticulous, and I always try to strive for the end goal and do not like to leave things hanging. I really like to build things – both physical and digital – and see the progress of my work; That's what inspires me the most in software engineering. Currently my plan consists of continuing my studies with Master's degree and specializing probably in Mechatronics and Machine Automation, but you never know where I will end up.
